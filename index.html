Regarding Roses

Roses are a gift from nature. So pretty. Soft, delicate petals. That magical feeling when they're in full bloom and their fragrance fills the air. In this video series we'll explore some of the ways roses are handled, retaining their essence even in rough, industrial settings.

Rose harvesting at its most elegant. Gathered by hand and crafted into beautiful necessities.

Delicate Contrast

Bare feet stomp soft petals into heavy vats. Water is added and the mixture heated for distillation, leaving pure rose perfume.

Florally Speaking

Although floriographic fluency is less common now, roses remain a universal gesture. A clear message, even when the code is long forgotten.
